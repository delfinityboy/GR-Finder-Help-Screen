Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions

How do I install GR Finder? ⌄
1. Go to the Chrome Web Store and search for GR Finder.
2. Click Add to Chrome → Add Extension.
3. The GR Finder icon will appear in your Chrome toolbar.
4. Open your Awaiting Payment page and click the icon to start.

Your 7-day free trial starts automatically on first install — no credit card required.
How do I search and tick invoices? ⌄
1. Open your Awaiting Payment page.
2. Click the GR Finder icon in your toolbar.
3. Type or paste invoice numbers — one per line:
INV-S-23-07-1015
INV-S-23-07-0891
INV-S-23-07-0889
4. Click Search — all matching invoices are ticked instantly.
5. See the results panel to confirm which invoices were found.
What does Tick / Untick do? ⌄
Search — finds and ticks invoices that match your list.
Tick — same as Search, ticks all matching invoices.
Untick — deselects all matching invoices from the page.

All three buttons show you a results panel so you know exactly which invoices were found.
An invoice shows "Not Found" — what should I do? ⌄
If an invoice shows ❌ Not Found, try these steps:

1. Refresh the page — the most common fix.
2. Check invoice number spelling — make sure it matches exactly (e.g. INV-S-23-07-1015).
3. Make sure you're on the Awaiting Payment tab — not All or Draft.
4. The invoice might be on a different page — scroll down or increase items per page.
How does the 7-day free trial work? ⌄
Your free trial starts automatically the first time you install GR Finder.

• No credit card required
• Full access to all features for 7 days
• After 7 days, the extension will show an upgrade screen
• Upgrade for $3/month to continue using GR Finder

The trial badge in the extension header shows how many days are left (e.g. "Trial: 5d left").
How do I upgrade to Pro? ⌄
1. Click Upgrade — $3/month on the paywall screen, or visit our store.
2. Complete the payment on Gumroad.
3. You will receive a license key by email in the format: GRF-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX
4. Open GR Finder → click Enter license key → paste your key → click Activate.
5. Done! Badge will show Pro ✅
I paid but didn't receive my license key — what do I do? ⌄
1. Check your spam/junk folder first.
2. Wait up to 24 hours — keys are sent manually.
3. If still not received, contact us at delfinityboy@gmail.com with your Gumroad order number.
We will send your key as soon as possible.
The extension opens but shows a blank screen — what do I do? ⌄
This usually happens when you're not on the correct page.

• Make sure you are on the Awaiting Payment tab.
• The extension only activates on the correct accounting page.
• If you see "Open Awaiting Payment page first!" — navigate to the correct tab first, then open GR Finder again.
The extension says "Could not connect. Refresh the page!" ⌄
This means GR Finder lost connection to the page. Simply:

1. Refresh the page (press F5 or Ctrl+R).
2. Wait for the page to fully load.
3. Click the GR Finder icon again.

This usually fixes the issue instantly.
Does GR Finder store or send my invoice data? ⌄
No. GR Finder does not collect, store on any server, or transmit any of your invoice data or financial information.

Your invoice numbers are only saved locally in your browser (Chrome storage) so the extension can restore your last session. This data never leaves your device.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

GR Finder ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you use the GR Finder Chrome extension.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

GR Finder does not collect, transmit, or share:

  • Invoice numbers or financial data
  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history or page content
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Account credentials of any kind

2. Local Storage Only

GR Finder saves the following data locally in your browser only using Chrome's built-in storage API:

  • Your last entered invoice numbers (so they restore on next open)
  • Your trial start date (to calculate remaining trial days)
  • Your license activation status (Pro or Trial)

This data is stored only on your device and is never sent to any external server.

3. Permissions Used

GR Finder requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab — to interact with the currently open page
  • scripting — to find and tick invoice checkboxes on the page
  • storage — to save your invoice list and trial status locally
  • tabs — to detect which page is currently open
  • host_permissions (*.accounting-platform.com) — to run only on the Awaiting Payment page

No permissions are used beyond what is necessary for the extension to function.

4. Third-Party Services

GR Finder uses Gumroad for payment processing. When you purchase a Pro license, you interact directly with Gumroad's platform. GR Finder does not receive or store your payment details. Please review Gumroad's Privacy Policy for more information.

5. Children's Privacy

GR Finder is not directed to children under 13 years of age and does not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: delfinityboy@gmail.com
